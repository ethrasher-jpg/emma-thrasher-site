Who I Am

The Story

Hey, I'm Emma. I picked up my first board as a kid and never looked back. Skating taught me everything worth knowing — how to fall, how to get back up, and how to commit. Every scrape is a story, every trick is a triumph. Life's too short to play it safe — so I don't.

What I'm About

When I'm not carving concrete or chasing the perfect line, I'm into everything that keeps the energy going. Music, street art, late-night drives, building things with my hands. I live loud and move fast.

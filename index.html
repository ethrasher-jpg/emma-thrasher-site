Who I Am

The Story

Hey, I'm Emma. I stepped on a skateboard before I could ride a bike — and by the time I was six years old, I was the best skateboarder in the United States. No exaggeration, no asterisk. While other kids were learning to tie their shoes, I was landing kickflips and dropping into halfpipes twice my height.

What I'm About

Growing up as a prodigy on four wheels meant skating became my whole world early. Competitions, demos, magazine covers — all before middle school. But it was never about the trophies. It was about the feeling. That rush when the board locks in and gravity just stops arguing with you. These days I'm still chasing that feeling in everything I do — music, street art, late-night drives, and building things with my hands.
